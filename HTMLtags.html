HTML START-UPS

*HTML headings*

- Headings are defined by the h1 tag.

This is heading 1

This is heading 2

This is heading 4

This is heading 5

*HTML paragraphs*

- Paragraphs are defined by the p tag.

This is a paragraph.

This is another paragraph.


*HTML links*

- Links are defined by the a tag.

This is a link

*HTML images*

- Images are defined by the img tag.



*HTML buttons*

- Buttons are defined by the button tag.


*HTML lists

An Unordered list is defined by the ul tag.

  • Coffee
  • Tea
  • Milk

An Ordered list is defined by the ol tag.

  1. Coffee
  2. Tea
  3. Milk
